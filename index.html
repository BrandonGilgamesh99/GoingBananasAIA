GOING BANANAS

How to Play

The goal of the game is to catch as many bananas for your monkey as possible in one minute.

Tilt the device left and right to move the monkey.
Tap the monkey to throw a rock and aim at the bananas on the tree to knock them down. When the bananas fall, catch them!
But watch out... there are banana peels on the jungle floor... if your monkey runs into one, it will trip and lose a banana!

Super Simple Interface

Download the game and get ready to tilt your phone and play!
